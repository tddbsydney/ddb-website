Volkswagen

When is a car more than a car?

We needed to remind people that Volkswagen is entrenched in Aussie culture.

We asked when is a car more than a car? →

As the brand name suggests, this is the people’s car – a legacy that transcends sheet metal and machinery. This was the thinking that gave rise to the campaign line “When is a car more than a car? When it’s a Volkswagen.”

But for the message to really hit home, it couldn’t come from us. We reached out to real Volkswagen owners and asked them to share what made their cars more than a car. The result; a joyous and honest celebration of life in a Volkswagen.
