The Sydney Opera House

Live response social, powered by a smart application of technology.

We needed to get more people inside the Sydney Opera House during summer.

8.2 million people visit the site of the Sydney Opera House every year.

Most take photo if the outside and move on, making it the most Instagrammed icon in Australia. People were a stick away. We selected photos using computer vision, inviting them inside with Instagram.

Solution

Turn photos of the Sydney Opera House on Instagram into incredible experiences inside by inviting users to #comeonin.

“Have a look inside, because this is where it all happens”

— Jan Utzon, Architect

126 unsuspecting Instagrammers invited to #comeonin.

How we found the photos

Custom software uses computer vision to identify photos of the famous sails along with geo-location to find ones posted in close proximity. An on-site response team picks an Instagram user for an experience and quickly film and posts a video response inviting them inside.

Results

In 4 weeks, over 5 million people saw a new side to the Sydney Opera House: the inside.

  • 126 unsuspecting Instagrammers invited to #comeonin.
  • Social engagement up 30% from campaign launch
  • 17 million impressions generated.
